WWinter Olympic Games in Sochi.2014.

This data visualization was created to compare most successful in figure skating counties countries. Data is related to Winter Olympic Games in Sochi, Russia in 2014.
1st chart is showing women champions and also predictions for 2022 Winter Olympic games.
Please note that number 3 is Gold, number 2 is Silver medal and number 1 is Bronze medal
2nd chart is showing total medals count by event and total medals won by team Russia.
3rd chart is showing total Gold medals by country
4rth chart is breaking team Russia medals by classes
